Task Description

Welcome to the negotiation task! In this task, you will be asked to negotiate over the price of a craigslist item with a randomly assigned partner. You can receive a bonus for a successful negotiation, and an additional bonus for getting close to your target price.

Before you start this task for the first time, please read the Negotiation Instructions using the link above. Additionally, please note the following:

This HIT is part of a scientific research project. Your decision to complete this HIT is voluntary, and your responses are anonymous. Your 14-digit AMT Worker ID is only collected by us for purposes of distributing payment to you, and will not be associated with any of your responses. The results of the research may be presented at scientific meetings, published in scientific journals, or made publicly available to other researchers. By clicking on the 'Submit' button for this HIT, you indicate that you are at least 18 years of age and that you consent to complete this HIT voluntarily.

Once you are ready, accept the task to begin. Thank you in advance for your time!
